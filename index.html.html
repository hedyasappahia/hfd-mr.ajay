Dear Dad,

Happy Father's Day to the best dad ever.

No words are enough to tell you how much you mean to us. Whenever life gets a little difficult or we're confused about what to do, you're always the person we look up to.

Despite the childhood you had, you made sure that we had one worth flexing about. You gave us so many experiences, so much fun, and so much exposure. Even when things got difficult, you never let us feel it. You made sure we got the best education, went on amazing trips, ate the best food, watched the best movies, and had opportunities that many people only dream of. We don't think we'll ever be able to thank you enough for that.

One thing we've always admired about you is that no matter what life threw at you, you never gave up. And that's exactly what you've taught us too. To keep going, no matter how hard things get.

We hope that one day we become the kind of people you can proudly point to and say, "Ye hain meri betiyan," to everyone who ever doubted you or looked down on you. We want to be your biggest achievement.

Thank you for not being like other parents. Thank you for trusting us, understanding us, and loving us so much. Thank you for never being like everyone else. Thank you for loving us so fiercely and unconditionally. Thank you for setting our standards so high that we know our worth and never settle for less than we deserve. Most importantly, thank you for simply being you.

Most of all, thank you for being you.

Thank you for every trip, every tasty meal, every movie night, every life lesson, every sacrifice, and every memory. Thank you for working so hard so that we could have a life full of opportunities and happiness.

We might not say it often, but we notice everything, and we appreciate everything.

And Dad, we promise you this: one day, we will make you proud.

We love you to Pluto and back.

Love, Your daughters ❤️
